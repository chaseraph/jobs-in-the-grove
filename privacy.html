The fine print

Privacy policy.

What we collect, what we do with it, and how to get it back out. Written in plain language because we’d rather you actually read it.

Effective April 23, 2026

Who we are

Jobs in the Grove is a community jobs board operated by News in the Grove, a local newsroom published by Firestarter Media. When this policy says “we,” that’s us.

What we collect

When you submit a resume

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and city.
  • A short professional headline and your skills / experience summary.
  • The resume file you upload (PDF or Word document).
  • Your preferences: job types, job categories, earliest start date, and commute range.

When you post a job

  • Company name, contact name, contact email, and contact phone.
  • Optional company website.
  • The job details you enter: title, type, category, location, compensation, description, and how to apply.

When you pay for a listing

  • Payment card details are handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.
  • We store the Stripe session ID so we can confirm your payment and match it to your listing.

When you browse the site

  • Our hosting provider and our API receive your IP address and browser user-agent in standard server logs, used for security and debugging.
  • We do not run analytics scripts, ad pixels, social tracking, or cookie-based tracking on this site.

How we use it

  • Resumes are made available to local employers looking to hire in our coverage area. Submitting a profile is an opt-in to that. When an employer responds to you, they receive the contact info you supplied.
  • Job listings are published on this site. Featured tier listings are also included in our newsletter and pinned to the top of the board.
  • Emails may be used to confirm a payment, let you know your listing went live, or follow up about your submission. We do not add you to marketing lists without your consent.
  • We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers.

How long we keep it

  • Resumes: 12 months from the date you submit, after which we automatically delete them. If you’d like yours deleted sooner, email us and we’ll remove it within 7 days.
  • Job listings: active on the public board for 30 days, then removed from the live board. We may retain a non-public archive for our own records.
  • Payment records: kept as required by applicable tax and accounting law, typically up to 7 years.

Third parties we rely on

We use a small, deliberate set of third-party services to run the jobs board. Each of these has its own privacy policy.

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Firestarter News API (jobs-api.firestarternews.com) — our backend server, operated by our parent company Firestarter Media, where resumes and job listings are stored.
  • GitHub Pages — serves this static website.
  • Google Fonts — serves the typefaces used on this page.

Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Ask for a copy of the personal data we have about you.
  • Ask us to correct something.
  • Ask us to delete your data.
  • Opt out of the resume database.
  • Withdraw consent for any specific use.

Oregon residents may have additional rights under state law. Email us and we’ll honor your request and confirm when it’s done.

If you’re an employer who received a resume through this board, you’re responsible for handling it under your own privacy practices once it’s in your hands.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not set tracking cookies. It may briefly use your browser’s storage to remember form fields if you refresh the page. No third-party advertising or analytics scripts are loaded.

Children’s privacy

This board is intended for adults seeking employment. We don’t knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a minor has submitted information here, email us and we’ll delete it.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect submitted data — HTTPS in transit, access limited to staff who need it, and payment data handled entirely by Stripe. No online system is perfectly secure, and we can’t guarantee absolute security, but we’ll tell you promptly if something goes wrong.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes, we’ll update the effective date at the top of this page and note the change on the jobs board home page.

Contact

Questions, requests, or corrections? Email chas@newsinthegrove.com. We’re a small newsroom, and a real human will read it.

About

Jobs in the Grove is a community jobs board from News in the Grove. Listings come from local employers — not scraped, not syndicated.
